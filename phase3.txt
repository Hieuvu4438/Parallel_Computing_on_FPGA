Layer 2 (Transient Detection): ZCR + Amplitude → Detect Crackles
Layer 3 (Spectral Screening): MFCC → Spectral anomalies
Early-exit mechanism: Thoát khi đạt consensus hoặc confidence > τ
CNN fallback: Signal khi cần deep analysis
